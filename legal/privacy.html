中文

AI模型領航員是非官方、獨立開發的 AI 模型決策工作台，提供模型比較、API 成本試算、離線 Token 粗略估算與產業動態，與 OpenAI、Anthropic、Google、Meta、xAI 等公司均無關聯。

  • 我們幾乎不蒐集任何個人資料。App 不要求註冊、不建立帳號、不追蹤您的身分。
  • 工作台資料：您建立的專案名稱、用途、必要條件、模型選擇、用量、成本參數、採用決策與備註，皆儲存在您的裝置上，不會傳送給我們。
  • Token 長度估算：輸入文字只在裝置上以模型中立的近似規則處理，不會上傳。估算不是任何特定模型 tokenizer 的精確結果。
  • 內容資料：App 從伺服器下載公開的新聞摘要 JSON。此過程僅涉及一般網路連線資訊（如 IP 位址），由內容傳遞主機依其標準作業處理，我們不另行記錄。
  • 訂閱：Pro 訂閱完全由 Apple App Store（StoreKit）處理。我們不接觸您的付款資訊。
  • 本機偏好：語言、收藏、追蹤與通知偏好等設定只存在您的裝置上。
  • 第三方服務：Apple App Store（訂閱與付款）、內容傳遞主機（下載摘要 JSON）。

聯絡：apple@jikker.net

English

AI Model Navigator is an unofficial, independently developed AI model decision workspace for model comparison, API cost planning, offline rough token estimates, and industry updates. It is not affiliated with OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, Meta, xAI, or any other company.

  • We collect almost no personal data. No registration, no accounts, no identity tracking.
  • Workspace data: project names, use cases, requirements, selected models, usage and cost assumptions, adoption decisions, and notes are stored on your device and are not sent to us.
  • Token length estimates: input text is processed only on your device with a model-neutral approximation and is never uploaded. It is not an exact tokenizer result for any specific model.
  • Content: the app downloads public digest JSON from a server. Standard connection metadata (e.g. IP address) is handled by the content delivery host per its normal operations; we keep no separate logs.
  • Subscriptions: Pro subscriptions are handled entirely by the Apple App Store (StoreKit). We never see your payment details.
  • Local preferences such as language, saved items, watchlists, and notifications are stored only on your device.
  • Third parties: Apple App Store (billing), content delivery host (digest JSON).
